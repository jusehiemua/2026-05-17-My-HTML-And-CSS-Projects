HTML Tags Challenge

The <article> Tag

The <article> tag is a self-contained, independent piece of content that makes sense on its own, like a blog post or a news story.

Here is an example

Topic: Why Dogs Make Good Pets

Here's how it is written on a webpage:

Why Dogs Make Good Pets

Dogs are loyal animals. They can provide friendship, protection, and happiness to many families.

The <details> Tag

The <details> tag creates a section that the user can open and close. It is useful when you want to hide extra information until the user clicks it.

Click here to see more information about dogs

Dogs need food, water, exercise, training, and love.

The <summary> Tag

The <summary> tag gives the visible heading for a <details> section. The user clicks the summary text to show or hide the hidden content.

The <time> Tag and datetime Attribute

The <time> tag is used to mark a date or time in a webpage. The datetime attribute gives the browser a machine-readable version of the date or time.

My next pet care appointment is on May 23, 2026.

The pet shop opens at 9:00 AM.
